# Activities

Since the establishment of the Khyentse Center (KC) various activities,
including conferences, workshops, projects, and lectures have been conducted by its members and collaborating colleagues,
focusing on various issues concerning the evolution of Buddhist scriptures and the formation of the Buddhist canons.
The following is a selection of such activities that in one way or another are connected to the initiation of BuddhaNexus
and to its development ever since.
